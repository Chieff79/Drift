## Full Description — ~500 words

```
Drift is a fast, safe internet accelerator for phones, tablets and computers. One tap builds a protected tunneled connection between your device and the nearest data centre, so everything you do online becomes faster, steadier and invisible to anyone sharing the network with you.

WHY DRIFT

— Public Wi-Fi in cafés, airports and hotels is usually open and untrusted. Drift encrypts your traffic, so banking apps, email and messengers stay private even on a stranger's network.
— When travelling, the path to your home cloud storage, work email or IPTV service becomes unpredictable. Drift smooths out the route and returns the speed you are used to.
— Streaming platforms — Netflix, Spotify, YouTube Music, Apple TV — play smoothly, without buffering or dropped frames, thanks to the nearest edge node and intelligent routing.

KEY FEATURES

• One tap to a protected network.
• Modern authenticated encryption (ChaCha20-Poly1305, AES-GCM) — the same algorithms used by banks and enterprise messengers.
• Automatic selection of the lowest-latency node.
• Built-in speed test: measure before and after you connect.
• Per-app split routing: send browser and streaming traffic through Drift, keep banking apps on the regular connection.
• Desktop TUN mode: one toggle secures every app on the system.
• Dark theme, system tray, quick tiles and widgets.
• Autostart on system boot and auto-connect on untrusted Wi-Fi.

SECURITY AND PRIVACY

Drift is designed around a zero-PII principle: we never collect your name, phone, email or address. To link your subscription we use only a Telegram identifier — nothing else. We do not keep a log of the sites you visit and we do not store your IP. Full details are in the privacy policy.

WHO IT IS FOR

— Travellers who need a steady link to home services.
— Freelancers and remote workers who live in coffee shops and coworkings.
— Families who want children's devices protected on public networks.
— Streamers and music lovers who care about smooth, drop-free playback.

PLATFORMS

iOS 14+, Android 8+, macOS 12+, Windows 10+, Linux (Ubuntu 22.04+, Fedora 38+). One account works on all your devices.

PLANS

A free basic profile with throttled speed; Premium with full speed and access to every edge node; Premium+ with a router configuration export for Keenetic and OpenWRT so you can protect the whole household at once.

SUPPORT

Telegram channel with updates and live support: @driftvpn.
Website and contact details in the About section inside Drift.

Drift is an accelerator and a shield, built so you can simply use the internet and stop thinking about it.
```

## Privacy (App Privacy — App Store Connect)

### Data Not Collected

In the "Data Collection" section select:

> **The developer does not collect any data from this app** — except for one optional identifier (see below).

If the form requires detail, fill it in as follows:

| Data Type | Purpose | Linked to User | Used for Tracking |
|-----------|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Other — Subscription Identifier (Telegram ID) | App Functionality | Yes (to user's subscription) | No |
| Crash Data | App Functionality, Analytics | No | No |

Every other category — **Not Collected**: Name, Email, Phone, Address, Photos, Contacts, Browsing History, Search History, Location, IDFA, Device ID, etc.

User-facing sentence for the listing:

> "Drift does not collect personal data. A Telegram identifier is used solely to link your subscription; the history and content of your connection are never recorded."
